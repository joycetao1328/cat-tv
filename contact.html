Our Staff

Sean P. Brown | Program Director | sean@columbiaaccess.tv

Sean became a member of the CAT staff in May 2011 to oversee station programming and equipment check-out. Five months later, he became Program Director of CAT. Among other things Sean spends his CAT time scheduling programming, teaching classes, assisting members and working on media service projects. Sean has a great passion for music, film and community as evidenced by his contributions to the Citizen Jane Film Festival and the True/False Film Festival. Sean has been a freelance writer, producer and musician for nearly 20 years. His credits include working with Space Station Media, MTV, HBO, CBS Sports, Nickelodeon, Google/YouTube, The Flaming Lips, The Polyphonic Spree, Kentucky Finance, Movies of Music and creating the show open music for the 2009 revival of MTV Unplugged. Sean is the proud father of a teenage son and is a fan of thoroughbred horse racing.
